The Alliance High School Endowment Fund(AHSEF) is a US 501(3)(c) charitable organization. All gifts to the fund from US tax payers are tax deductible.

Paypal

Gifts can be made through Paypal by clicking the button below.

Gifts collected through Paypal will be transferred to the Vanguard account on a quarterly basis. Please note there is an associated Paypal transaction fee of 2.2% + $0.30.

Payroll deductions

Many US employers support their employees donating to their preferred causes through payroll deductions. If donations to AHSEF are not yet available through payroll deductions at your company, please get in touch, secretary@ahsef.org and we'll work with you to set it up.
