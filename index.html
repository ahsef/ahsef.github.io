Empowering Excellence at Alliance High School

The Alliance High School Endowment Fund (AHSEF) is a US 501(c)(3) charitable organization founded to promote academic and overall student excellence at Alliance High School, Kenya.

Our Four Funds

Academic Scholarship Fund

Awards scholarships to students based on academic merit.

Sports Directed Fund

Provides financial support for team sports, encouraging excellence in academics and athletics.

Need-Based Scholarship Fund

Seeded by a generous Old Boy, awards scholarships to needy students.

AHS Chapel Project Fund

Supports the construction of a multi-purpose hall for events and programs.

501(c)(3)

All gifts to AHSEF in the US are tax deductible and eligible for employer matching.
